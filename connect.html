We are The Juice

Juice, founded in 2006 in Poland, is one of the leading, independently owned design and postproduction companies in the region, with offices in Wrocław, Warsaw, and Tokyo. Our 70+ creatives located worldwide, make up a wealth of talent, highly experienced VFX and CGI artists, multi-media specialists, art directors, designers and sound designers, specialized in animation, compositing, color grading, concept art and character design. We’re helping some of the most influential brands to become stronger. Our client list includes BMW, Coca-Cola, Crispin Porter + Bogusky, EA, Fiat, History Channel, Nike, Mitsubishi, SABMiller, Stink London, Toyota, T-Mobile, Virgin Mobile.
